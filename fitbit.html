Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the collar battery last?
The KitFit+ Collar features dual batteries providing up to 7 days of continuous monitoring on a single charge.
Is the collar waterproof?
Yes, the collar is IP67 rated - fully protected against dust and can withstand temporary submersion in water.
Can I monitor multiple cats?
Absolutely! Each cat gets their own collar and profile. The app supports unlimited pets with one subscription.
What if my cat loses the collar?
Use the GPS and UWB tracking in the app to locate the collar. The Find My Collar feature works indoors and outdoors.
